Flexgrow

The Premier Cannabis Service Provider

At FlexGrow, we're not just adapting to the future of cannabis cultivation – we're actively shaping it. In a world racing towards exponential automation, we recognize that the true differentiator lies in understanding the cultivator. Our approach marries the power of artificial intelligence, machine learning and blockchain technology with deep cultivator insights.

The 6 pillars below will be at the core of every decision made at FlexGrow - and will guide our path to revolutionizing the cannabis industry:

Embracing Merging Technologies

Our AI-driven systems continuously analyze and optimize growing conditions, predicting trends and potential issues before they impact your yield. With machine learning algorithms processing data from multiple sources, your cultivation environment is fine-tuned in real-time for peak performance.

Innovation

Experience unparalleled yield with our proprietary Pi-Wall breeding technology. Our container units, producing up to 80 kg per year, integrate cloud connectivity for remote monitoring and control. This allows for immediate adjustments based on AI-generated insights, ensuring optimal growth conditions 24/7.

Blockchain-Based Ecosystem App

Our decentralized Proof of Stake (POS) blockchain ensures immutable data storage and integrity. This technology not only enhances transparency and traceability but also streamlines regulatory compliance. Our ecosystem app serves as a centralized hub, connecting cultivators directly with retail cannabis users, all secured by blockchain technology.

Unwavering Compliance

Navigate complex regulations with confidence. Our systems provide real-time access to verified cultivation data, ensuring full regulatory compliance and simplifying the license acquisition process. By leveraging blockchain for data integrity, we establish an auditable trail that meets and exceeds regulatory requirements across various jurisdictions.

Operational Excellence

Our proprietary software suite places human capital at the forefront, enabling seamless cross-departmental collaboration without the need for traditional KPIs. This innovative approach not only optimizes workflows but also creates an environment that attracts and retains top industry talent, ensuring you're always working with the best.

Customer Excellence

Experience support that goes beyond the ordinary. Our AI-powered customer service is augmented by deep insights into behavioral psychology, providing you with intuitive, responsive assistance. From predictive maintenance alerts to personalized cultivation advice, we ensure you have the support you need, precisely when you need it.

Strategic Partners

Delta 9

Delta 9 was founded by a father and son team in 2012. Bill and John Arbuthnot obtained a production license under the Marihuana for Medical Purposes Regulations in December 2013. One year later, in March 2014, they reached another milestone: securing a license to distribute medical cannabis, making Delta 9 the fourth Licensed Producer of medical cannabis in Canada. Since that time, Delta 9 has grown from a team of 2 to a team of 300+ employees, and the company operates a 185,000 sq. ft. cannabis facility with over 300 Pods in production in Winnipeg, Manitoba.

Branchenverband Cannabiswirtschaft

The voice of the cannabis economy in Germany represents all industry segments and company sizes in dialogue with political bodies and administrations. Its key focus areas include recreational cannabis regulation, industrial hemp, medical cannabis, and technology, trade & services. Tasks involve active participation in expert councils and committees within ministries and attending hearings in the German Bundestag or Federal Government. Bringing together industrial policy, technology, and economic expertise, the organization advocates for improved political conditions, guiding regulatory changes at all levels. Events are organized to engage directly with decision-makers and drive necessary industry reforms.
